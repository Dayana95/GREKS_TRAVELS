León Viejo Ruins

Includes

Includes express private transportation with air conditioner, from Augusto César Sandino airport, from Managua to san Jorge, Ferry Tickets from San Jorge to Moyogalpa Port, trasportation in the island, guide service, tickets, transportation to Moyogalpa Port, transportation from Moyogalpa Port to San Jorge and transportation from San Jorge to Managua.

What to bring?

Sun glasses, video or photo camera, repellent, sun cream, 3 or 4 liters of purified water, hiking shoes, jeans or shorts and hat or cap.

Tour Description

León Viejo Ruins, also called León Viejo is the place where the city of León was originally founded, located in the town of Puerto Momotombo (Lake port in the lake of Managua), minucipality of La Paz Centro, department of León, located in Nicaragua, Central America. Because of the constant earthquakes caused by the Momotombo volcano its residents after a cabildo, decided to abandon it to seat in another site near to the indigenous people of Sutiaba
